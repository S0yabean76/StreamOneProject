A very warm welcome from the 3 Lovely Ladies!


We're 3 mums who liked to sing amongst ourselves just for fun. Well, mostly for the tea and cake involved!


Then Heather saw an advert on the telly for Stand Up to Cancer - a fundraising event to challenge yourself to do something that terrifies you - and promptly thought of singing in public! As we're nice people, we couldn't possibly let her go it alone so the 3 Lovely Ladies were born!


We hope you have as much fun browsing our site as we had creating our music! Have a listen to some of our tracks or grab a cuppa, stay awhile and watch some videos!


And if you like what you see then why not book us for your function?! Take a look at our contact us page for details.
